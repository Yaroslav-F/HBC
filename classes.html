Classes

Ladies Class (Mon - 9am)

A lot of girls use boxing for fitness. It’s a great way to get into shape and gain many benefits:

  • enhances cardiovascular health
  • increases total body strength
  • increases muscle mass and tone
  • increases hand eye coordination
  • decreases stress and lower disease risk
  • improves body composition
  • reduces the risk of Alzheimer’s

Boxing is the ultimate challenge. There is nothing that can compare to testing yourself the way you do every time you step in the ring.

Sugar Ray Leonard

In boxing, it is about the obsession of getting the most from yourself: wanting to dominate the world like a hungry young lion.

Anthony Joshua

Punch Bags Class (Mon, Wed - 8pm; Fri - 7pm)

Working with heavy bags improve your aerobic fitness; improve power; improve coordination and core stability; improve boxing techniques; develop self defence skills; improve body shape; decrease stress. Heavy bag workout helps in training and building your muscle mass. Along with that, it also focuses on the cardio training. So while you are performing an intense heavy bag punching workout your heart is beating faster and faster. This raises your heart rate and speed the circulation making your heart strong. You have to stay alert and cautious throughout. This improves the coordination and sense of balance.

Integration Class (Tue, Thu - 7pm)

One of the world’s oldest and most celebrated sports, boxing, has proven to be popular among many people, for a variety of reasons. Boxing has become a global phenomenon with tremendous cross-over appeal. It has the potential to turn lives around by providing focus and an outlet for pent up aggression. Boxing’s greatest benefit might be in the training and preparation needed to reach the fighting level.
Integration Boxing Class will help you to join your technique into one skilful fight.

Pads Class (Wed - 7pm, Fri - 6pm)

Working on pads help boxers develop speed, accuracy and endurance. Your partner wears the pads on his hands to create a moving target that you attack with various combinations of strikes. They can be used to develop both hand and foot strikes so your training more closely resembles a real fight. One of the main benefits of using pads is that they can be used in sparring to simulate the variability of an actual fight. Training on the pads is one of the best ways to develop your fighting reflexes, punching technique, defence technique, and fighting strategy.

Sparring Class (Thu - 8pm)

Cuz D’Amato, the famous trainer of champions, once said: “ The hero and the coward both feel the same thing, but the hero uses his fear, projects it onto his opponent, while the coward runs. It’s the same thing, fear, but it’s what you do with it that matters. “Sparring class will help you to become the hero and use your skills in the fight.

Hard training prepares fighters to perform well in any competition. We provide a number of classes for all levels of ability from absolute beginners to those with years of experience. If you train hard you will be hard to beat. Our classes run evenings and weekends, we also offer one to one sessions.
