Facilities

Heathrow Boxing and Mixed Martial Arts Club welcomes everyone to join us. You will find great fighting spirit, unique training techniques and professional coaches. It is an opportunity to build up your strength and find a comprehensive fitness program tailored to your individual needs to focus on one or more specific skills.

Boxing & MMA

We offer a full size boxing ring, various types of punch bags and MMA cage training. We provide high quality, effective equipment.

Our equipment caters to the needs and interests of individuals with a variety of goals, from general fitness, weight loss and toning to sport performance. Changing rooms and locker facilities are also available.

Backyard

We provide backyard facilities for some of the more authentic boxing training routines including rope and tractor tyres.
